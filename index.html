Birds

Birds are fascinating creatures that grace the skies with elegance and freedom. From the tiny hummingbird to the majestic eagle, their diversity is astounding. Birds communicate through songs and calls, each species having unique vocalizations. Their feathers offer insulation, flight, and brilliant displays.

Eagle

Eagles are powerful birds of prey known for their sharp vision, strong talons, and impressive wingspan. Found in many parts of the world, they symbolize freedom, strength, and courage in different cultures. With eyesight up to eight times sharper than humans, eagles can spot prey from great distances. They feed mainly on fish, small mammals, and other birds, swooping down with incredible speed and accuracy. Eagles build large nests, called eyries, high in tall trees or on cliffs. Majestic and graceful in flight, these birds are admired as kings of the sky and protectors of the natural world.
